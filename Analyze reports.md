# 📊 Microsoft Defender XDR – Reports (Summary)

## 📌 Overview
The **Reports blade** in Microsoft Defender provides:

- Security insights  
- Trend analysis  
- Visibility across threats and environments  

✅ Purpose:
Monitor security posture and identify risks across:
- Devices  
- Email  
- Identity  
- Applications  

---

## 🧠 Key Concept

✅ Reports = **Security Visibility & Insights**

- Show what’s happening in your environment  
- Help identify trends and risks  
- Support decision-making  

---

## 🖥 Where to Access

Step 1 > Go to Microsoft Defender portal  
Step 2 > Navigate to Reports
