# ⚙️ Microsoft Defender XDR – Configure Portal Settings (Summary)

## 📌 Overview
The **Settings** page in the Microsoft Defender portal allows you to:

- Configure Defender products  
- Manage notifications  
- Control security behaviour  

✅ Primary focus:
Email notifications for security events  

---

## 🧠 Key Concept

✅ Configuration = **Operational Awareness**

- Ensure the right people are notified  
- Enable timely response to threats  

---

## 🖥 Where to Access

Step 1 > Go to Microsoft Defender portal  
Step 2 > Navigate to Settings  
Step 3 > Select Microsoft Defender XDR  
Step 4 > Open Email notifications  

---

## 📩 Notification Types

### 🚨 Incident Notifications

- Sent when new incidents are created  
- Helps SOC teams respond quickly  

---

### 🔍 Threat Analytics Notifications

- Sent when new threat intelligence reports are available  
- Helps track emerging threats  

---

## ⚙️ Configure Incident Notifications

Step 1 > Settings > Defender XDR > Email notifications  
Step 2 > Select Incidents  
Step 3 > Click Add incident email notification  
Step 4 > Enter:
- Name  
- Description  
Step 5 > Define criteria (device / alert filters)  
Step 6 > Add recipient email addresses  
Step 7 > Click Create  

✅ Result:
Team receives alerts for new incidents  

---

## ⚙️ Configure Threat Analytics Notifications

Step 1 > Settings > Defender XDR > Email notifications  
Step 2 > Select Threat Analytics  
Step 3 > Click Create notification rule  
Step 4 > Enter:
- Name  
- Description  
Step 5 > Define threat criteria  
Step 6 > Add recipient email addresses  
Step 7 > Click Create  

✅ Result:
Team receives updates on threat intelligence  

---

## 📌 Benefits

- Real-time incident awareness  
- Better communication within SOC team  
- Faster response to threats  
- Improved visibility of emerging risks  

---

## ✅ Summary Flow

Configure Settings > Set Notification Rules > Define Criteria > Notify Team  

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaways

- Defender portal settings control security notifications  
- Email alerts keep SOC teams informed  
- Incident notifications = operational awareness  
- Threat analytics notifications = strategic awareness  
- Proper configuration improves response time  

---
