# 🔍 Microsoft Defender XDR – Investigate Incidents (Summary)

## 📌 Overview
The **Incident page** provides a complete view of an attack, allowing SOC analysts to:

- Analyse alerts  
- Understand attack flow  
- Investigate impacted entities  
- Take response actions  

✅ Goal:
Turn alerts into actionable investigation  

---

## 🧠 Key Concept

✅ Incident Investigation = Understanding the **full attack story**

- How it started  
- How it spread  
- What it impacted  
- What needs to be fixed  

---

## 🧭 Investigation Sections

---

## 📊 1. Incident Overview

✅ Snapshot of key information:

- Attack categories (MITRE ATT&CK aligned)  
- Scope (affected users/devices)  
- Alerts timeline (chronological view)  
- Evidence summary (artifacts + remediation status)  

✅ Use:
Quick triage and prioritisation  

---

## 🚨 2. Alerts

Step 1 > View all related alerts  
Step 2 > Check severity + source  
Step 3 > Review timeline  

✅ Shows:
- Attack progression  
- Source (Endpoint, Identity, Email)  
- Reason alerts are linked  

---

## 💻 3. Devices

Step 1 > View impacted devices  
Step 2 > Select device  
Step 3 > Open device timeline  

✅ Investigate:
- Processes  
- Events  
- Suspicious behaviour  

---

## 👤 4. Users

Step 1 > View affected users  
Step 2 > Open user profile  

✅ Investigate:
- Suspicious sign-ins  
- Identity risks  

---

## 📧 5. Mailboxes

- Analyse phishing emails  
- Review email activity  
- Investigate email-based threats  

---

## ☁️ 6. Apps

- Identify affected applications  
- Analyse suspicious cloud activity  

---

## 🤖 7. Investigations (Automation)

Step 1 > Open Investigations tab  
Step 2 > Review automated actions  
Step 3 > Approve pending actions  

✅ Uses:
Automated Investigation & Response (AIR)  

---

## 🧾 8. Evidence & Response

✅ Shows all entities with status:

- Files  
- Processes  
- Emails  

Each item has:
- Verdict (Malicious / Suspicious / Clean)  
- Remediation status  

✅ Helps decide next actions  

---

## 🧠 9. Incident Graph

✅ Visual attack map showing:
- Entry point  
- Devices impacted  
- File activity  
- Relationships between entities  

Step 1 > Select node  
Step 2 > View details  
Step 3 > Investigate connections  

✅ Goal:
Understand attack flow visually  

---

## 💥 10. Blast Radius Analysis

✅ Advanced view of:

- Current impact (post-breach)  
- Potential future spread (pre-breach)  

Step 1 > Select entity  
Step 2 > View blast radius  
Step 3 > Analyse attack paths  

✅ Shows:
- Lateral movement paths  
- Critical assets at risk  

✅ Use:
Prioritise containment actions  

---

## 🔄 Investigation Flow

Open Incident > Overview > Alerts > Devices/Users > Evidence > Graph > Respond  

---

## ✅ Key Takeaways

- Incident page = central investigation workspace  
- Provides full visibility across attack lifecycle  
- Graph + timeline show attack progression  
- Blast radius helps predict spread  
- Automation speeds response  

---

## 🔐 Security Insight

👉 Good investigation = Context + Correlation + Action  

(Not just reacting to individual alerts)
